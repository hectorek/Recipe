
Ingredients
===========

Before you begin, make sure you have the following ingredients:


+--------------+-------------+
|**Ingredient**| **Amount**  |
+--------------+-------------+
|   Potatoes   |  x3 Large   |
+--------------+-------------+
|   Eggs       |  x6 Large   |
+--------------+-------------+
|   Onions     |  x1 Large   |
+--------------+-------------+
|   Olive oil  |    20ml     |
+--------------+-------------+
|   Salt       |    10mg     |
+--------------+-------------+


Utensils
========

You should also bring out the following kitchenware:

.. |knife| image:: /images/knife.jpg
           :scale: 5 %

* 1 Chopping board |board|

.. |board| image:: /images/board.jpg
           :scale: 5 %

* 1 Potato peeler |peeler|

.. |peeler| image:: /images/peeler.png
            :scale: 15 %

.. admonition:: Note

   The size of the frying pan will determine the diametre of your omelette.
   If you want to make several small omelettes I recommend you use
   a large frying pan to deep-fry the potatoes and a small one to shape your
   omelette in the :ref:`last stage <platingstage>`.
